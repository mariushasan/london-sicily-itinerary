# London Itinerary - April 18-20, 2026

---

## Day 1 - Saturday, April 18 (Afternoon Arrival)
*Area: South Bank & City of London*

### 1. [Borough Market](https://maps.app.goo.gl/e2FgX4ex8vL53uKz9)
- **Time:** Head here first upon arrival (closes 5pm)
- **What:** London's most famous food market - street food, artisan produce, baked goods
- **Address:** 8 Southwark St, SE1 1TL

### 2. [St Dunstan in the East Church Garden](https://maps.app.goo.gl/eX7QKBkSWSpz8Qjg7)
- **Time:** Late afternoon
- **What:** Stunning ruined church overtaken by climbing plants - beautiful photo spot
- **Address:** St Dunstan's Hill, EC3R 5DD
- **Walk:** ~15 min from Borough Market along the Thames

### 3. [Tower Bridge](https://maps.app.goo.gl/SA4YRfXmiKAWmLov6)
- **Time:** Early evening
- **What:** Iconic bridge - walk across and enjoy the views
- **Address:** Tower Bridge Rd, SE1 2UP
- **Walk:** ~10 min from St Dunstan's

### 4. [The View from The Shard](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xs7PEmWuH3WL51gQA)
- **Time:** Sunset slot recommended (around 7:30-8pm in April)
- **What:** London's highest viewing platform - 360-degree panoramic views
- **Address:** 32 London Bridge St, SE1 9SG
- **Walk:** ~15 min back west, or take the Tube (1 stop)
- **BOOK IN ADVANCE**

## Day 2 - Sunday, April 19
*Area: East London morning, West End afternoon & evening*

### 1. [Columbia Road Flower Market](https://maps.app.goo.gl/94APP41btkvAh7in6)
- **Time:** 8:00-11:00am (market runs 8am-3pm, best early)
- **What:** Vibrant Sunday flower market - one of London's best street experiences
- **Address:** Columbia Rd, E2 7RG

### 2. [The Garden at 120](https://maps.app.goo.gl/D1uisT7PfvF3vHFm8)
- **Time:** Late morning
- **What:** Free rooftop garden with great City views (15th floor, 120 Fenchurch St)
- **Address:** 120 Fenchurch St, EC3M 5BA
- **Travel:** ~20 min south by bus/Tube from Columbia Road
- **Note:** May have reduced weekend hours - check ahead

### 3. [The British Museum](https://maps.app.goo.gl/aiBTrp8Zv6kGRLJr6)
- **Time:** Early afternoon (opens 10am, closes 5pm)
- **What:** World-class museum - Rosetta Stone, Elgin Marbles, Egyptian mummies. Free entry
- **Address:** Great Russell St, WC1B 3DG
- **Travel:** ~20 min by Tube (Central line from Bank to Tottenham Court Rd)

### 4. [Seven Dials Market](https://maps.app.goo.gl/aMZKoPGNzwPG7pki9)
- **Time:** Mid-afternoon
- **What:** Indoor food hall in Covent Garden - great for a late lunch or snack
- **Address:** 35 Earlham St, WC2H 9LD
- **Walk:** ~10 min south from British Museum

### 5. Chinatown
- **Time:** Late afternoon
- **What:** London's Chinatown on Gerrard Street - bubble tea, dim sum, bakeries, vibrant atmosphere
- **Address:** Gerrard St, W1D (Leicester Square area)
- **Walk:** ~5 min from Seven Dials Market

### 6. [Soho](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ewaf8tRVPcyPK34c6)
- **Time:** Evening
- **What:** Wander the lively streets, bars, restaurants, and nightlife
- **Address:** Soho, W1
- **Walk:** Directly adjacent to Chinatown - just keep exploring north

## Day 3 - Monday, April 20
*Area: Royal London & West London, then back east*

### 1. [Buckingham Palace](https://maps.app.goo.gl/vhT8sijjcxzbWmJw5)
- **Time:** Arrive by 10:30am for the Changing of the Guard (11am on Mondays)
- **What:** The King's official residence - watch the guard ceremony from the Victoria Memorial
- **Address:** Buckingham Palace, SW1A 1AA

### 2. [Kensington Gardens](https://maps.app.goo.gl/zoY3Xk5ZJHVwfRaRA)
- **Time:** Late morning
- **What:** Beautiful royal park - Kensington Palace, the Peter Pan statue, Italian Gardens
- **Address:** Kensington Gardens, W2 2UH
- **Walk:** ~25 min west through Green Park & Hyde Park (or bus)

### 3. [Kyoto Garden](https://maps.app.goo.gl/8a1F7o8mRsWevGij9)
- **Time:** Early afternoon
- **What:** Peaceful Japanese garden with a waterfall and koi pond inside Holland Park
- **Address:** Holland Park, Ilchester Pl, W8 6LU
- **Walk:** ~15 min west from Kensington Gardens

### 4. [Portobello Road Market](https://maps.app.goo.gl/L3zFLvw9Uijq2E1j8)
- **Time:** Afternoon
- **What:** Famous Notting Hill market street - vintage, antiques, food stalls
- **Address:** Portobello Rd, W11
- **Walk:** ~10 min north from Holland Park

> **Note:** The full market runs on **Saturdays**. On Monday, only permanent shops and a few stalls will be open. Still a charming street to walk, but expect a much quieter experience.

### 5. [Sky Garden](https://maps.app.goo.gl/fNoJpJxxFi5TPpjD6)
- **Time:** Late afternoon / sunset
- **What:** Free indoor garden and observation deck on the 35th floor of the Walkie Talkie building
- **Address:** 20 Fenchurch St, EC3M 3BY
- **Travel:** ~40 min by Tube from Notting Hill Gate to Monument
- **BOOK IN ADVANCE** (free but reservations required)
