# Day 1 - Palermo
*Area: Historic Centre & Western Palermo*

> **Check before you go:** Il Cambusone is closed Mon & Tue. Catacombe dei Cappuccini reopens at 3PM after midday closure. La Martorana closes at 1PM on weekdays.

---

### 1. [Palazzo dei Normanni](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Palazzo+dei+Normanni+Palermo)
- **Time:** 8:30AM (opens earliest - beat the crowds)
- **What:** The Palatine Chapel's gold mosaics are unmissable

### 2. [Villa Bonanno](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Villa+Bonanno+Palermo)
- **Time:** After the palace
- **What:** Lovely shaded garden - perfect breather before the Cathedral
- **Walk:** ~2 min from Palazzo dei Normanni

### 3. [Palermo Cathedral](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Palermo+Cathedral)
- **Time:** Opens 9:30AM, closes 1:25PM on weekdays
- **What:** Visit the roof terrace if you can!

### 4. [Mercato Ballaro](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Mercato+Ballaro+Palermo)
- **Time:** Morning is best
- **What:** Grab street food (pani ca meusa, arancini) for a snack as you wander

### 5. [Church of Jesus (Casa Professa)](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Chiesa+del+Gesu+Casa+Professa+Palermo)
- **Time:** Mid-morning
- **What:** Jaw-dropping Baroque interior - one of the most spectacular churches in Sicily

### 6. [Fontana Pretoria](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Fontana+Pretoria+Palermo)
- **Time:** Any time (open 24h)
- **What:** Impressive Renaissance fountain in a beautiful piazza

### 7. [La Martorana](https://www.google.com/maps/search/La+Martorana+Palermo)
- **Time:** Before 1PM on weekdays
- **What:** Stunning Arab-Norman mosaics inside - same piazza as the fountain

### 8. [Chiesa di Santa Caterina](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Chiesa+di+Santa+Caterina+Palermo)
- **Time:** Open until 5:15PM - pop in now or save for after lunch
- **What:** Same piazza! The monastery bakery has incredible cannoli

---

### Lunch: [Il Cambusone](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Il+Cambusone+Palermo)
- **Time:** 12:30-2:30PM lunch service
- **What:** Highly rated seafood pasta
- **Walk:** ~5 min north from the piazza
- **Closed Mon & Tue**

---

### 9. [Teatro Massimo](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Teatro+Massimo+Palermo)
- **Time:** Tours until 3:30PM
- **What:** Italy's largest opera house - Godfather Part III filming location!

### 10. [Catacombe dei Cappuccini](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Catacombe+dei+Cappuccini+Palermo)
- **Time:** Reopens at exactly 3PM after midday closure
- **What:** Haunting and unforgettable - no photos allowed inside
- **Walk:** ~20 min west (or grab a cab)

### Optional: [Mercato del Capo](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Mercato+del+Capo+Palermo)
- **What:** Right on the walk back from the Catacombs toward Teatro Massimo - a lively, authentic market

### 11. [Giardino Garibaldi](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Giardino+Garibaldi+Palermo)
- **Time:** Closes at 8PM
- **What:** Beautiful garden with Europe's largest ficus tree - wind down here after a big day

### Optional: [Palazzo Butera](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Palazzo+Butera+Palermo)
- **Time:** Open until 8PM (closed Monday)
- **What:** Gorgeous art museum with a rooftop terrace overlooking the sea - 2 min walk from Giardino Garibaldi
