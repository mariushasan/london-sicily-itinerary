# Day 3 - Palermo to Catania
*Travel day*

> **Tip:** Use the morning to see anything you missed in Palermo, or sleep in after two full days. The train ride is scenic - sit back and enjoy it.

---

### Train: Palermo Centrale → Catania Centrale
- **Duration:** ~3 hours (direct Intercity) or ~3.5-5 hours (regional with change at Messina)
- **Cost:** ~14-21 EUR (regional), ~25-35 EUR (intercity)
- **Tickets:** Buy on [Trenitalia](https://www.trenitalia.com/en.html) (app or website) or at the machines in the station
- **Tip:** The Intercity is direct and worth the few extra euros. If you take the coastal route via Messina, sit on the **right side** for sea views heading east, then **left side** for Etna views heading south to Catania
