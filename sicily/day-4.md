# Day 4 - Mount Etna & Catania
*Morning: Guided Etna tour · Afternoon: Free time in Catania*

---

### Morning: [Mount Etna Guided Tour](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Mount+Etna+Sicily)
- **What:** Guided hike on Europe's tallest active volcano — craters, lava fields, and massive views across eastern Sicily. Tours typically reach the mid-altitude craters (~2,500m) with a volcanologist guide
- **Tip:** Bring layers — it's significantly colder at altitude, even in spring. Sturdy shoes essential

---

### Afternoon: Free time in Catania
- Back in the city by early-mid afternoon. Revisit anything from Day 3, wander the backstreets, or just sit with a granita and watch the city go by
